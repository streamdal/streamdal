Streamdal is an open-source data observability graph and rule-based management
tool that helps you take action. It's built on a collection of several
open-source components - a server, an SDK, and a console UI - designed to
integrate seamlessly into your code.

# <center>_Why_ Streamdal?</center>

Streamdal is not just another <a class="pkeyword" href="/en/resources-support/glossary/#observability">observability</a>
or data management tool. It's a philosophy for a way of thinking and working
with data that prioritizes a few concepts:

- Real-time insights... that are _truly_ **real-time** and not _"real-time"_
- Flexibility
- Ease of use
- Preventing issues

Read more about this philosophy and design choice in our brief
[manifesto](https://streamdal.com/manifesto/).
