**Streamdal** is an _open-source data observability and governance tool_ built on a collection of several **Opensource** [components](../core-components/overview), [server](../core-components/server), [sdk](../core-components/sdk), and [console ui](../core-components/ui) that integrate seamlessly into your code.

- **Data Observability**: Think `tail -f` for your data!
- **Data Governance & Enforcement**: Keeping your data compliant.
- **Data Quality Monitoring**: Ensuring your data's accuracy.
- **Data Transformation**: Tailoring your data.
- **Data Obfuscation, Masking, and Stripping**: Securing your data.
