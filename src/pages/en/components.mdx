## Keyword Highlighting

Keyword highlighting is for glossary terms. This is accomplished through 
assigning classes:

- <span class="pkeyword">Class: pkeyword without the hoverstate is achieved with a span</span>
{/* */}
- <a href="#" class="pkeyword">Keyword links have a special hoverstate</a>

  ...but require `<a href>` and will not work with `[markdown](syntax)` for links.

- <span class="ykeyword">Class: ykeyword. No hover state, just for extra 
highlighting for statements or special titles</span>
  - **<span class="ykeyword">Here is the same highlighting, but in bold</span>**
